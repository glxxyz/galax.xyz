Teletext50



(please excuse the ugliness of this hastily put together page, I just want to share the fonts...)

This font is a very small tweak to the excellent Bedstead, that switches the smooth diagonal lines for 70s/80s style SAA5050 generated (hence the name '50') characters. All of the hard work was done by the people who built Bedstead and they deserve all of the credit for this font.

It's not as useable as a font as Bedstead, but has different more retro feel, for recreating Teletext pages as they would have looked. For more information about how this font was generated see the original Bedstead pages. The tweaked source that I used is at github.com/glxxyz/bedstead

Click here for a PDF that shows the whole character set.
